'Heard' UX Research

'Heard' is an online platform that matches clients with therapists. The platform allows therapists to pick the clients that they want to work with and make it easy for clients to find a therapist that works best for their needs. The team is trying to find a way to reduce the time and effort that both therapists and clients are spending to find their needs.

Task

Develop user personas that 'Heard' could target based on the user interviews and surveys.
