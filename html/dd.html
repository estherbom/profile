TASK TWO

UI/UX Design

Main features include search listings, add listings, make an offer, sign up as professional, and order services.

Our potential users are divided into three groups: sellers, buyers, and professionals.

USER EXPERIENCE

Define the requirements with the founder to create guidelines to share with product manager and the team, conduct research, sketch out solutions to make user stories and flow chart, rapidly produces wireframes and prototypes, and conduct usability testing or A/B testing.

USER INTERFACE

Develop responsible web design layouts and create assets that are integrated with the overall design components.

We ask professionals to fill in the sign-up form, so we can verify their information as well as give them our customize rating before they can be listed on our platform.

Questions to keep in mind:

• How might we make the sign up process
as simple as possible?

• How might we display the information
that the users provided in the user dashboard?

Professional Sign Up & User Dashboard

Add Listing

Add listing form collects general information of the property that will reflect on the property detail page and the information needed for Make an Offer feature.

Questions to keep in mind:

• How can we make the form less complicated?

Order Services

Two different user flows are created: one is for professionals who are providing the services, and the other for buyers who are purchasing the services.

Questions to keep in mind:

• How do professionals add their services?(required fields are different depending on the service types)

• How might we help buyers find services that they want to order?

Make an Offer

Make an Offer is the most complicated feature to create. Conducted multiple usability testings and repeated the UX cycle to get the best design result.

Importance of Flowchart

If the process required more than one user, having a clear flowchart will help create a strong prototype.
