Day 5 - Validate

Interviewed 5 potential users, who have experienced searching for recipes online, with high-fidelity prototype.

Add Ingredients

Problem: ** Search bar and ‘Add Ingredients’ are confusing** Users had a difficulty connecting two features

Suggestion: If the focus is on search, the search bar should be on top. If the focus is on the ingredients, the ingredient list should be on top of the search.

Displaying Substitute Ingredients

Problem: Need to be more obvious that users can click on the ingredients to display substitute ingredients.

Suggestion: put how many substitute ingredients are there: '(2)'
