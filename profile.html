ARTIST'S
STATEMENT

There was a time in my life when I felt like
everyone was progressing towards their dreams
except for me. No matter how much effort I put forth,
I was simply spinning my wheels, while the rest of the
world spun faster and faster around me. I believe
that this helpless feeling of being stuck in a cocoon
is something everyone experiences at one point or another.
It is that feeling that I am portraying through my practice.
The concept of practices is to capture a mood of isolation
and take a step back with it to reveal how universal it really is.
